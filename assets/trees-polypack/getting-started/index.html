Getting Started

Welcome to the Trees Poly Pack asset!

You can import the asset into your project and get started right away with dragging the trees into your scene!

Importing the Package

The asset pack is made for Unity and can be imported directly into your project from Unity Asset Store.

However, it should work in all engines, with potentially slightly different setup on the materials.

Adding Trees to Your Scene

After importing, you will find the asset content under Trees PolyPack > Prefabs.

Simply drag and drop any prefab into your scene. Each prefab is already set up with the correct materials and ready to use out of the box.

Customizing Materials

Materials are located in Trees PolyPack > Materials. You can freely adjust colors and properties to match your project's art style.

All models are setup with a 2 material setup. One material goes for the top of the tree and the other for the trunk. You can adjust the colours individually to fit the environemnt of your game. The UVs are setup to work well with a grading texture. A few are provided in the project in Materials folder. Simply set it up in Albedo field. You can adjsut the offset and tiling for different results, or input a custom texture.
