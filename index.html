A little bit about me // Just a bunch of yap
I began studying game development back in 2019, and using Unity enginge I made my first game called Cool Strike which made Valve shake their boots and start development of CS2. It was then that I realized how powerful of an influnce I already have on this industry (That totally did happen, true story)

I went to College and University, putting in years of learning and practice, and in the end managed to land a job where I did my course, becoming a Game Development Lecturer. In that time, I have become a qualified teacher and mastered the basics through teaching of all parts of development inside of Unity. After 3 years of teaching I made the decision to focus on myself and pursuing a career as an Indie Game Developer. In 2025 I made my first commercial game together with a friend of mine. Last Term released on 7th of October 2025. This marks my journey as an Indie Developer and it is first of many games to come. If you want to support me and help deliver quality games, buy my games on Steam or check out my assets if you are a fellow developer. You can also follow me on Twitter or YouTube for updates and behind-the-scenes content.
